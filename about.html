About This Resource

Why this OER exists, who it helps, what it is not designed to do, and where first-time visitors should begin.

Purpose

Menstrual prediction research sits between medicine, digital health, data science, and product design. This OER is not meant to replace the literature. It is meant to lower the entry barrier while preserving rigor, so readers from different backgrounds can understand what the others are actually talking about. A concise research-oriented snapshot is on the Research Case Study page.

Who it helps / what it is not for

Best for

  • Cross-disciplinary onboarding
  • Research design and problem framing
  • Understanding product limits, data realities, and evaluation pitfalls

Not for

  • Replacing medical advice
  • Delivering an off-the-shelf commercial blueprint
  • Reducing the area to a single “best model” recommendation
