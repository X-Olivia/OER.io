About this OER

This OER accompanies the project “An Embodied Care Robot Supported by Physiological Menstrual Cycle Inference.” It provides a structured survey of existing work, taxonomies, performance limits, and research directions; open datasets and data-processing code; and the interim report, implementation notes, and preliminary results. The aim is to support reuse, citation, and further work in menstrual prediction and cycle inference, especially under sparse sensing and embodied deployment.
